ABOUT COMPANY AGRICULTURE
 
AGRICULTURE IN THE DESTRUCTIVE STAGE 
 

Present atmosphere, the land under cultivation for long ago, which were used for producing sufficient food materials for the millions of people in the world, is now being converted and used for the construction of Buildings, Shopping Complex, Factories, etc., thereby the agriculture is being deteriorated gradually.

Due to the changing climate condition, millions of acres of cultivable land in the world are changing into dry and barren land and becoming as desert. Further, the natural calamities like flood, cyclone, etc. are also deteriorating the agriculture.

By global warming, the snow is dissolving in the countries like Antartica thereby the sea level becoming high and the water enter into the low level cultivable land causing the deterioration of agriculture.

Over and above, there is heavy loss in the agricultural profession, as the poor farmers are using very small pieces of land for cultivation and further the cost of cultivation is also increasing, therefore farmers are getting out of agricultural profession.

Now, The United Nations Organization is warning the world countries that we will have to face severe food crisis, if such a situation continues in the forthcoming years.

REMEDIES
 

Therefore, it is the responsibility of every citizen to save the agricultural profession from the deterioration condition

The important remedy to solve the above problems, is Company Agriculture. The Company Agriculture Method is implemented successfully and profitably in the countries like Africa, America, Australia, etc. Now, in INDIA, our organization “GOOD VISION” entered into the field by following the above Company Agriculture Method with simple commercial and technical plan for the resurrection of agriculture and solution to socio- economic problem.
