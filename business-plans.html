BUSINESS PLAN
HOW TO JOIN US AS AN ADVISOR
 
  • He/she should have completed 18 years.
  • He/she should be an INDIAN citizen.
  • He/she should join as an Advisor and Customer as well at the same time.
BENEFITS
 
ADVISOR BENEFITS:
 
  • Commission from Direct Business
  • Commission from Team Business
  • Monthly Leadership Bonus - MLB
  • Rewards
  • Generation Income (or) Pension
SALIENT FEATURES:
 
  • Income through Investment
  • Low Business Volume
  • No Time limit to attain the benefits
  • Online Business Update
DIRECT BUSINESS COMMISSION
 
Every Field Worker/Agent earn 1st year commission on his/her business as per the Plan as follows : 
 
Installment Plan
 
  • 4% on - 12 months (1 year)
  • 10% on - 42 months (3 ½ years)
  • 20% on - 66 months (5 ½ years)
  • 22% on - 84 months (7 years )
    From 13th month onwards commission will be paid as per the schedule of the company.
Single Installment Plan
 
  • 4% on - 42 months (3 ½ years)
  • 8% on - 60 months & 72 months (5 years & 6 years)
  • 10% on - 96 months & 120 months (8 years & 10 years)
